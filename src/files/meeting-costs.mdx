##### Backend

- AWS Lambda function written in Javascript.
- Infrastructure with Serverless Framework.
- Runs a cron job to get the most recent exchange rates.

##### Frontend

- Made with React, Redux, and Redux-Saga.
- Styled with Tailwindcss.
- Deployed with Netlify.
